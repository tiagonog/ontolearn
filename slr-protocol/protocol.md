# Systematic Literature Review Protocol

Conducted following **PRISMA 2020** (Page et al., 2021) with scope defined via the **PICOC** strategy. Managed with the **Parsifal** tool. Search executed between **Sep/2022 and Feb/2023**, covering publications from **2008 to 2022**.

Result: 2,447 records initially retrieved → 28 primary studies after screening and Quality Assessment.

## Research Questions

- **RQ1**: How is the assessment of specific competencies performed in Virtual Learning Environments (VLEs)?
- **RQ2**: Which techniques and tools are used to analyze specific competencies in VLEs?

## Databases

ACM Digital Library, IEEE Digital Library, ScienceDirect, Scopus, Wiley Online Library.

## Search String

Applied to title, abstract, and keywords (syntax adapted per database):

```
(virtual learning environment OR learning management system OR
 network-based tool OR collaborative virtual environments OR
 Online Learning Environment OR e-learning system OR distant learning system)
AND
(evaluation OR assessment OR exercise OR quiz OR exam OR questionnaire OR big data)
AND
(competencies OR skills)
AND
(data analysis OR learning analytics OR educational analytics OR
 learning data mining OR educational data mining OR academic data mining OR dashboard)
```

## Inclusion Criteria (at least one)

- **IC1**: Studies identifying or describing how specific competencies are assessed in VLEs.
- **IC2**: Studies presenting, proposing, or evaluating techniques/tools for analyzing specific competencies in VLEs.

## Exclusion Criteria (any)

- **EC1**: Not written in English or Portuguese.
- **EC2**: Restricted (paid) access or not fully accessible.
- **EC3**: Duplicates across databases.
- **EC4**: Invited papers, keynotes, workshop reports, theses, or dissertations.
- **EC5**: Incomplete documents, drafts, slides, or extended abstracts.
- **EC6**: No direct adherence to competency assessment/analysis in VLEs after full reading.

## Quality Assessment (QA)

Checklist of 5 questions, each scored 1.0 (yes) / 0.5 (partial) / 0.0 (no); cut-off score: **3.0/5.0**. Scores assigned by the main researcher and sample-reviewed by the advisor; discrepancies > 1.0 rediscussed until consensus.

- **QA1**: Clearly defined and grounded research objectives?
- **QA2**: Assessment/analysis technique described in enough detail for comprehension or replication?
- **QA3**: Educational context and VLE well detailed?
- **QA4**: Empirical evaluation, experiment, or formal validation present?
- **QA5**: Results and conclusions clearly presented and supported by data?

## Data Extraction Form

Per selected study: (1) sample; (2) educational context (higher education, company training, secondary/primary school); (3) research area (AI, data model, system architecture); (4) assessment tool (quizzes, essays, etc.); (5) data-analysis technique (adaptive learning, IRT, etc.); (6) key concepts (for ontology class mapping); (7) relations between concepts (for object properties and axioms).
